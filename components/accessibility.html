Accessibility Statement

Tibbling is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We continually improve the user experience for everyone, and apply the relevant accessibility standards.

Conformance status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) defines requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities. This website conforms to WCAG 2.1 level AA. Conformance means that we've made an effort to meet but not necessarily satisfy all WCAG success criteria at the given level.

Measures to support accessibility

Tibbling takes the following measures to ensure accessibility:

  • Include accessibility as part of our mission statement.
  • Integrate accessibility into our procurement practices.
  • Provide continuous accessibility training for our staff.
  • Assign clear accessibility targets and responsibilities.
  • Employ formal accessibility quality assurance methods.
